About Us

In 1957, Mr Kunchammad Haji Koodathan Kandi started a restaurant near Central railway station in chennai and he named it as Central Restaurant .The restaurant grew, Why? The food was awesome. The service felt like home. And the customers were treated like family. And we've been delivering that same tasty food and service ever since.

At Central restaurant, we don't just serve food. We make people happy . With more than 64 years of experience under our belts, we understand how to best serve our customers through tried and true service principles.We create food we're proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.
